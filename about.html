About LUMEN

LUMEN Speech & Hearing is a clinic based in Hisar, Haryana providing evidence-based audiology and speech-language services. Our team comprises certified audiologists and speech-language pathologists committed to improving communication and hearing health for children and adults.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and families through accurate diagnostics, personalized therapy, and transparent hearing care solutions.

Unique features
  • Government-approved diagnostic tests (OAE, ABR)
  • Latest digital hearing aids — rechargeable & invisible models
  • EMI options on hearing aids
  • Home visits available on request
